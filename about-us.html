Who We Are

A Legacy Forged in Gold & Love

Shree Ram Ji Diamond Palace was founded in 1978 by Shri Ram Prakash Gupta in the heart of Dariba Kalan — Delhi's most revered jewellery market. What began as a small workshop with a single artisan has flourished into one of India's most trusted fine jewellery destinations.

For over four decades, we have adhered to one principle: never compromise on quality. Every ring, necklace, pendant and bangle that leaves our workshop is a testament to generations of mastered craftsmanship, ethically sourced materials and BIS hallmark certified purity.

Today, led by the second generation of the Gupta family, we blend traditional Mughal and Rajput design motifs with contemporary aesthetics — creating pieces that are as at home in a bridal trousseau as they are in a modern wardrobe.

Why Choose Us

Our Core Values

Certified Purity
Every piece carries the BIS hallmark — India's official guarantee of gold and silver purity. We never compromise on material quality, no matter the price point.
Master Craftsmanship
Our artisans train for years under master craftsmen. Each piece undergoes 14 quality checks before it is deemed worthy of wearing the Shree Ram Ji name.
Ethical Sourcing
We source all gemstones and metals from certified, conflict-free suppliers. Our supply chain is transparent, traceable, and responsible — always.
Customer First
Every customer is family to us. From personalised jewellery consultations to lifetime maintenance, we are with you long after the purchase.
Rich Heritage
Rooted in the Mughal jewellery tradition of Delhi, our designs carry centuries of Indian artistry. Heritage is not our past — it is our product.
Constant Innovation
We marry age-old techniques with modern CAD design, 3D modelling, and precision casting — bringing new life to timeless designs without losing soul.

Our Journey

Milestones Through the Years

1978
The Beginning
Shri Ram Prakash Gupta opens a small workshop in Dariba Kalan with two artisans, a dream, and unwavering dedication to purity.
1992
BIS Hallmark Certification
Among the first jewellers in Delhi to receive official BIS hallmark certification, cementing our reputation for uncompromising quality.
2003
Flagship Showroom
Opened our first full-scale showroom with 200+ live displays, inviting customers to experience our entire collection in one place.
2012
Diamond Collection Launch
Launched our exclusive Diamond Palace line, featuring conflict-free IGI-certified diamonds set in 18KT and 22KT gold.
2019
50,000 Customers
Celebrated serving 50,000 happy customers across India with a special heritage collection dedicated to our patrons.
2024
Digital Presence
Launched our online jewellery portal, bringing 1800+ designs to customers across India with free shipping and easy returns.
